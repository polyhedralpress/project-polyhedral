## Abilities
Every creature, including characters and non-player characters, has six
abilities that are used to describe its general aptitude, those abilities are:
**Strength**, **Agility**, **Vitality**, **Knowledge**, **Wits**, and
**Presence**.

These abilities govern how the creature interacts with the world around it.
Each ability has a score which translates into a rank&mdash;typically between
&minus;5 to +5, though some creatures may have ranks higher or lower than
these&mdash;that modifies the creature's dice pool for almost every action in
the game.

- **Strength (St)**, a measure of a creature's raw physical prowess, power, and
  endurance.
- **Agility (Ag)**, a measure of a creature's speed and manual dexterity.
- **Vitality (Vi)**, a measure of a creature's energy, stamina, and tolerance to
  pain.
- **Knowledge (Kn)**, a measure of a creature's understanding, reasoning, and
  memory.
- **Wits (Wi)** a measure of a creature's mental accuity, cleverness, and
  observation.
- **Presence (Pr)**, a measure of a creature's force of personality and social
  skills.

A character interacts with the world by performing actions. These actions may be
scaling a wall, searching for clues, or even making an attack. When you take an
action, if there is ever a chance of failure or the outcome is uncertain you
allow a roll of the dice to determine the result.
